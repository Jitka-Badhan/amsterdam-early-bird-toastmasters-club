A quick checklist

  1. Added to the AEBT Whatsapp group?
  2. Got a Welcome email from Toastmasters Europe Toastmaster automation site?

If either of these are not done, please contact the Vice President Membership.

As you continue with the toastmaster journey, you will learn to use two tools we work with, and that are provided by Toastmasters International.

They are:

  • Pathways BaseCamp, our learning and education platform to access all Pathways materials
  • EasySpeak, for scheduling speeches and meetings

What next?

  1. Access BaseCamp to choose your Pathways.
  2. Go to EasySpeak and mark your availability for the next few meetings.
  3. Your Vice President of Education (VPE) will assign roles to you for the upcoming meetings. Keep an eye open for emails that you receive, when roles are assigned to you!
  4. In the meanwhile, start preparing for your 1st speech - the "Ice breaker" speech. Please read the documentation here.
  5. When you are assigned a role, you can learn more about what's expected from you in the Roles Definition page.
  6. Remember this club only runs because of volunteer efforts! That means your level of participation shapes the club, it's culture and ultimately the quality of the club depends on YOU :)
