Club Roles

A successful club meeting requires good participation. There are several roles to be fulfilled. As a member of the toastmasters club, you are required to take a role when called upon. That is the toastmaster's promise. Frequent declining of meeting roles or last minute cancellation will earn you bad karma points.

Read here on how to sign up for roles.

Are you assigned a role and you don't know what you should be doing? This is the right place. The role definitions are mostly standard, but it is written from Amsterdam Early Birds' perspective.
